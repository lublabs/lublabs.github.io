## The client

A travel agency with a strong partner network that was handling booking operations manually.

## The problem

The business had valuable distribution relationships, but execution relied on phone calls, emails, and spreadsheets. This made growth expensive and operationally fragile.

The founder's opportunity was clear: build an internal platform first, then offer it to partner agencies as a SaaS product.

## What we built

LubLabs designed and developed **Flyx**, a multi-tenant B2B flight booking platform.

### Core features

- Multi-tenant architecture with isolated agency workspaces
- Flight search and booking workflows
- Third-party integrations with airline and GDS providers
- Booking lifecycle management (search, book, modify, cancel)
- Payment flow support
- Revenue and performance analytics
- Group booking workflows

### Technical challenges solved

- Integrating enterprise-grade flight distribution APIs
- Designing secure tenant isolation and access controls
- Managing real-time pricing and availability data
- Coordinating booking and payment workflows under strict reliability constraints

## Current status

Flyx is in internal testing before broader rollout across the agency's partner network.

LubLabs continues as the technical partner for ongoing releases and scale-up work.

## Why this matters

Service businesses with operational complexity can productize workflows into software and create new revenue channels.

This is where LubLabs provides end-to-end support: product direction, architecture, and execution.
