## The client

A startup in the rehab and recovery space. Client identity remains confidential.

## The challenge

The team needed to move quickly from idea to a production-ready product that athletes and recovery-focused users could actually use daily.

They needed one app experience across iOS and Android without maintaining two separate native codebases.

## What we built

LubLabs designed and developed **Gametime Rehab**, a cross-platform mobile app focused on structured rehab workflows.

### Core delivery scope

- Product and feature scoping for the first launch milestone
- UX architecture for daily routines and progress tracking
- React Native app development for both iOS and Android
- Backend and API integration for user data and plan updates
- Launch hardening for store-readiness and production reliability

### Technical outcomes

- Single React Native codebase powering both platforms
- Faster iteration speed for post-launch releases
- Consistent product experience across iOS and Android
- A stable launch baseline that supports growth and feature expansion

## Current status

The app has launched and is actively used while we continue iterative improvements with the founding team.

## Why this matters

For early-stage startups, a cross-platform launch strategy can reduce time-to-market while still delivering a polished user experience.

This project demonstrates how LubLabs helps founders take an idea to a shipped product with practical engineering decisions from day one.
