# Case Study: From Manual Bookings to a SaaS Platform

## How LubLabs Helped a Travel Agency Build a Scalable B2B Flight Booking Product

---

## The Client

A travel agency with an established network of smaller agency partners across the region. They had been managing flight bookings manually — a process that worked when small, but couldn't scale, couldn't be monetized, and left money on the table every day.

---

## The Problem

The agency had something valuable: relationships with other travel agencies who needed to book flights for their customers. But everything was manual — phone calls, emails, spreadsheets. There was no system, no visibility, and no way to grow without adding headcount.

More importantly, the founder saw an opportunity: if they could build software to power their own booking workflow, they could white-label it and sell it to other agencies as a SaaS product — turning an operational cost into a new revenue stream.

They needed a technical partner who could take that vision and actually build it.

---

## What We Built

LubLabs designed and developed **Flyx** — a multi-tenant B2B flight booking platform that allows travel agencies to search, book, and manage flights for their customers in one place.

**Core features:**

- Multi-tenant architecture — each agency gets their own isolated workspace
- Smart flight search powered by the **Sabre GDS API** — one of the world's largest travel distribution systems
- Direct integration with **FlyDubai** for expanded route coverage
- Full booking management — search, book, modify, cancel
- Payment processing built in
- Real-time analytics dashboard — revenue metrics, booking performance, customer insights
- Group booking support

**The hard technical problems we solved:**

- Integrating Sabre's enterprise-grade API, which requires deep domain knowledge of the travel industry's GDS infrastructure
- Building a true multi-tenant system where agency data is fully isolated and secure
- Handling real-time pricing and availability across multiple airline sources simultaneously
- End-to-end payment processing within a regulated travel booking context

---

## Where It Stands Today

Flyx is currently in internal testing ahead of its public launch. The platform is live, being used and validated by the founding agency's own team before opening to their network of partner agencies.

This is an ongoing engagement — as Flyx launches and scales, LubLabs continues as the development partner for new features, integrations, and infrastructure.
